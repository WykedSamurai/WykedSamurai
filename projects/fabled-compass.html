Flagship Project

Fabled Compass

A professional development and hiring platform designed to help people prove what they can do, not just describe it on a resume.

Early Alpha — Not Released for Public Use

The Problem

Traditional resumes rely heavily on claims. Candidates say they have skills, and employers are left to interpret those claims through job titles, bullet points, interviews, and assumptions.

That system can overlook people with real ability, especially when their experience does not fit neatly into a conventional resume format.

The Solution

Fabled Compass is designed to help professionals build a Living Professional Record that grows over time through workplace scenarios, assessments, evidence, projects, certifications, and employer-verified activities.

The goal is to give professionals better ways to demonstrate ability and give employers more structured information for hiring and evaluation.

Platform Concept

Core parts of Fabled Compass

Living Professional Record

A growing professional identity that stores evidence of learning, accomplishments, projects, assessments, certifications, scenarios, and verified activities.

Workplace Scenarios

Structured professional situations that allow members to demonstrate judgment, communication, problem-solving, leadership, and role-specific skills.

Evidence-Based Hiring

A hiring approach that gives employers more than a resume by allowing them to review candidate evidence, completed scenarios, and structured skill demonstrations.

For Professionals

  • Create a professional identity beyond a traditional resume
  • Complete realistic workplace scenarios
  • Document projects, certifications, and accomplishments
  • Build evidence of skills over time
  • Show employers proof of ability

For Employers

  • Create opportunities
  • Design workplace scenarios and assessments
  • Review candidate evidence
  • Use structured information during evaluation
  • Reduce overreliance on resumes alone

Current Stage

Fabled Compass is in its very beginning alpha stage. The current focus is concept validation, landing page development, business card support, selective feedback, and early platform planning.

Founder Connection
