Project Concept

Wyked Samurai Consulting

A workplace development concept using tabletop roleplay, scenario design, and facilitated exercises to help teams practice leadership, communication, trust, and conflict navigation.

Concept in Development — Not Released for Public Use

Core Idea

Wyked Samurai Consulting is built around the idea that people learn workplace skills more deeply when they practice them in realistic, structured, low-risk scenarios.

Instead of relying only on lectures or slides, the concept uses roleplay-based situations to help participants make decisions, communicate under pressure, and reflect on team behavior.

Learning Focus

  • Leadership behavior
  • Communication and listening
  • Trust-building
  • Conflict navigation
  • Hybrid and cross-functional teamwork
  • Ethical decision-making

Possible Deliverables

What this project may become

Session Templates

One-shot and multi-session workplace scenarios designed around leadership, communication, and team decision-making.

Facilitator Toolkit

Guidance for running structured learning sessions, asking reflection questions, and connecting roleplay outcomes to workplace behavior.

Participant Materials

Character sheets, scenario prompts, reflection pages, and workplace skill trackers designed for professional development use.

Development Notice

Wyked Samurai Consulting is a development-stage concept. The materials, names, workflows, scenario structures, and documentation are not released for public use.
