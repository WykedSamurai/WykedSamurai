Private Platform Concept

Private TTRPG Hub

A private website concept for playing and organizing Pathfinder, Star Wars Saga Edition, Dungeons & Dragons, and other tabletop games with friends.

Concept in Development — Not Released for Public Use

Core Idea

The Private TTRPG Hub is envisioned as a campaign space where friends can play multiple tabletop systems, manage characters, organize campaign information, track continuity, and support roleplay with structured tools.

The project is separate from Fabled Compass, but it shares Wyked Samurai's broader interest in scenario design, structured records, and interactive storytelling.

Possible Systems

  • Pathfinder
  • Dungeons & Dragons
  • Star Wars Saga Edition
  • Custom roleplay systems
  • Solo and group campaign support

Possible Features

What the hub may support

Campaign Records

Campaign bibles, session notes, NPC records, house rules, locations, factions, and timeline tracking.

Character Tools

Character profiles, background pages, build notes, relationship tracking, and system-specific support.

AI-Assisted Support

Private continuity questions, NPC-building chats, campaign guidance, roleplay support, and reusable setting information.

Development Notice

The Private TTRPG Hub is a development-stage private platform concept. It is not released for public use, and its planning materials remain part of the Wyked Samurai development umbrella.
